Bijection Proof: Odd vs Even Subsets

Set S = {1, 2, 3, 4}. Prove that the number of odd-sized subsets (Left) equals the number of even-sized subsets (Right) by finding the unique corresponding pair.
The bijection is defined by **flipping the membership of the element 4**:
If 4 is **IN** the subset, the matched subset is found by **removing 4**. If 4 is **NOT IN** the subset, the matched subset is found by **adding 4**.
