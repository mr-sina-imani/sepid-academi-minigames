Grid Path Traversals (4 x 5 Grid)

The grid has $N=4$ rows and $M=5$ columns. You must make exactly **4 Up (U)** moves and **5 Right (R)** moves to reach the destination. Every path corresponds to a unique string of 9 letters (e.g., U R R U...).

Use $\rightarrow$ (Right) and $\uparrow$ (Up) keys to move.
